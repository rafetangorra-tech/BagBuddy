THE PROBLEM
03/14
THE PROBLEM

THE HEAVY BAG
HASN'T HAD A COACH.

Most fighters train alone. The bag is patient. It will let you stand there doing the same lazy 1-2 for an hour. Nobody calls combos. Nobody runs your round clock. Nobody pushes you through the last thirty seconds.

01
YOUTUBE IS PASSIVE
Tutorials teach technique. They don't time your rounds, call your combos live, or push you when you're gassed.
02
APPS ARE BLOATED
Fitness apps bury the one thing fighters want — the bag workout — under gamification, subscriptions, and tap-tap UX you can't use mid-round.
03
GYMS ARE GATED
Private coaching is $80/hr. Group classes aren't bag work. Home training gets you 80% there — if you have structure.
04
SWEATY HANDS, TINY SCREENS
You can't tap a phone mid-round. Any workout that requires looking at a screen breaks the flow of real bag work.
