Investor Overview — April 2026
The Problem
The Heavy Bag Is the Most Popular Training Tool in Combat Sports. It Has Zero Tech Support.
Millions of people train on heavy bags every day — in gyms, garages, and basements. But there's no coach standing next to them calling combinations. They end up throwing the same 3 punches on repeat, get bored, and quit.

Existing timer apps just count down rounds. They don't tell you what to throw, when to move, or how to train with purpose. They're glorified stopwatches.
The Solution
A Coach in Your Pocket.
Bag Buddy calls out combinations out loud — hands-free, eyes up, 100% focused on the bag. No staring at your phone. No guessing what to throw next. Just work.

Boxing and Muay Thai. Three training modes. 149 combinations. Coach audio drops between combos. Warning bells, round timing, and Apple Health tracking — all built in.
Product
Three Ways to Work.
  • Stand and Bang — Pure offense. Back-to-back strike combinations called out loud. No defense, just commit to every punch.
  • Stick and Move — Combos with defensive moves built in. Slips, rolls, pivots, checks. Train like you're in a real fight.
  • Drillers Make Killers — One complex combination, drilled for 60 seconds until it's muscle memory. Then rotate to the next.
  • Coach Audio Drops — Random motivational cues between combos. "Hands up." "Stay busy." "Push through." Like having a trainer in the room.
  • Music Integration — Spotify and Apple Music play through the session. Callouts duck the music automatically, then restore it between combos.
  • Apple Health & WHOOP — Every session logged as a boxing workout with calories. Syncs to WHOOP automatically via Apple Health.
