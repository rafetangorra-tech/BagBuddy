Train Like You Fight.
The hands-free heavy bag training app for boxers and Muay Thai fighters. Spoken combo callouts, coach audio, full round timing, Apple Health integration.
Investor Overview — April 2026
The Problem
The Heavy Bag Is the Most Popular Training Tool in Combat Sports. It Has Zero Tech Support.
Millions of people train on heavy bags every day — in gyms, garages, and basements. But there's no coach standing next to them calling combinations. They end up throwing the same 3 punches on repeat, get bored, and quit.

Existing timer apps just count down rounds. They don't tell you what to throw, when to move, or how to train with purpose. They're glorified stopwatches.
The Solution
A Coach in Your Pocket.
Bag Buddy calls out combinations out loud — hands-free, eyes up, 100% focused on the bag. No staring at your phone. No guessing what to throw next. Just work.

Boxing and Muay Thai. Three training modes. 149 combinations. Coach audio drops between combos. Warning bells, round timing, and Apple Health tracking — all built in.
Training Modes
Three Ways to Work.
Boxing and Muay Thai. Pick your discipline, pick your mode, and go.

Stand and Bang

PURE OFFENSE.

Back-to-back strike combinations called out loud. No defense — just commit to every punch and keep moving forward.

Stick and Move

HIT AND DON'T GET HIT.

Every combo includes a defensive move — a slip, roll, or cover. Strike and move like a smart fighter.

Drillers Make Killers

ONE COMBO. PERFECT IT.

A single complex combination drilled for 60 seconds until it's muscle memory. Repetition builds skill that holds up under pressure.

Features
More Than a Timer.
Everything you need to train with purpose. Nothing you don't.

Coach Audio Drops

Random motivational cues between combos. "Hands up." "Stay busy." "Push through." Like having a trainer in the room.

Music Integration

Spotify and Apple Music play through the session. Callouts duck the music automatically, then restore it between combos.

Apple Health & WHOOP

Every session logged as a boxing workout with estimated calories. Syncs to WHOOP automatically via Apple Health.

Streaks & History

Full session history with daily training streak tracking. Your coach checks in every day at 5 PM to keep you accountable.

Full Round Timer

1-12 rounds, 30 seconds to 10 minutes each. Configurable rest, warning bell, and three pacing speeds.

Haptic Feedback

Feel the round start, warning bell, and round end through vibration. Train with AirPods and never miss a cue.

Partner with professional fighters and celebrity boxing coaches to release signature combo packs — curated sets of their go-to combinations, recorded in their voice, available as premium in-app purchases.
