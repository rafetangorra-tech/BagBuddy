FAQ

ANSWERS.

IS IT ACTUALLY FREE?

Yes. No subscriptions, no in-app purchases, no ads, no tracking. The whole app — every mode, every combo, full history — is free.

WHY NO ANDROID?

Solo developer who fights, builds the app he uses. iOS first because that's what's in his gym bag. Android may follow if there's demand.

WHERE DO THE COMBOS COME FROM?

Curated by working coaches. Numbered notation matches what you hear in any boxing or Muay Thai gym — 1 = jab, 2 = cross, etc.

DOES IT WORK OVER MUSIC?

Yes. The combo callouts duck your music briefly, like a navigation app. You stay in your zone.
